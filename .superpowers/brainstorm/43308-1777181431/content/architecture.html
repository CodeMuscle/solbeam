Hard Disqualifiers — Vetoes regardless of score

Top-10 wallets hold > 70% of supply
Sniper wallets hold > 30% of supply
Dev wallet has sold > 50% of their allocation already
Same deployer address rugged 2 or more previous tokens
Bundled launch detected (coordinated multi-wallet first buy)
Mint authority NOT renounced on a token older than 10 minutes

Dynamic Exit Monitoring (Position Tracker)

🚀 MOONSHOT 10x from entry — Telegram alert fires, position highlighted in red to prompt exit
🏃 RUNNER 3–10x from entry — green alert, option to update take-profit
📈 MODERATE 1.5–3x from entry — soft notification
⚠️ BREAK-EVEN Price drops back to entry price + 0.5% (fees) — urgent Telegram alert to cut position
💀 STOP-LOSS Optional hard stop at -30% — configurable in Settings
